# Tommy Fernandez Photography - Site Plan

## Business Overview 

Tommy Fernandez is a freelance photographer based out of Miami, Florida and Orlando, Florida that specializes in Graduation photography, portraiture photography, urban photography, and commercial photography. 

**Target Audience:** Clients could range from young teens (16-18) to older adults (45-65) who need a photographer that specializes in one of Tommy's specialzed areas.

**Brand Personality:** Contemporary, sophistacated, sleek, organized, and ease of access to be able to market and cater to Tommy's wide ranging audience

## Page Structure

### 1. Home 

- Title section "Tommy Fernandez Photography" on top of background image 
- Small About Me section with space for a headshot 
- Quick links to specific portfolio galleries 

### 2. Gallery

- Galleries organized by category: Graduation, Portraits, Commercial, Urban
- Users will be to toggle between each gallery and display the gallery they wish to view

### 3. Contact and Booking

- Links to get in contact with Tommy, such as phone number, email, and links to social media 
- Form potential clients can fill out to get in contact with Tommy
- Book directly on the website for a photoshoot

### 4. About Me

- Headshot of Tommy
- Links to social media
- Content about the professional career of Tommy in photography and what he can offer

## Design Direction

**Colors:**

- Primary: #25692f
- Secondary: #0f0f0f
- Accents: #a61b1b 

**Typography:**

- Headings: Serif font (Clean, sophisticated, modern)
- Body: Sans-serif font (Clean, sophisticated, modern, and above all legible)

**Overall aesthetic:** Clean, sophisticated, modern, contemporary, yet inviting and organized
