About Me

Hi, I'm Tommy Fernandez, a freelance photographer based in Miami and Orlando, Florida. My journey with photography began during my high school years when I first picked up a camera for a class project. What started as a simple assignment quickly turned into a deep passion that would shape my future.

Throughout high school, I found myself captivated by the way a single photograph could freeze a moment in time and tell a story without words. I spent countless hours learning the craft, experimenting with different techniques, and developing my own unique style. Whether it was capturing candid moments of my friends or exploring the vibrant streets of Miami, I knew that photography was more than just a hobby—it was my calling.

After graduating, I made the decision to pursue photography as a career. It wasn't always an easy path, but my dedication and love for the art form kept me moving forward. Today, I specialize in graduation photography, portraiture, urban landscapes, and commercial work. Each shoot is an opportunity to connect with new people and help them preserve their most meaningful moments.

What drives me is the ability to create images that others can cherish for a lifetime. There's nothing more rewarding than seeing the joy on a client's face when they view their photos for the first time. I bring a contemporary and sophisticated eye to every project, ensuring that each image reflects not just a moment, but the emotion and story behind it.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring new locations, seeking inspiration in the world around me, or connecting with fellow creatives. I believe that great photography comes from genuine passion and a desire to see the beauty in everyday life.
